## 📱 Required Permissions (Setup Guide)
To make the auto-clicker work natively, Android requires you to explicitly allow SynapseClick two specific application permissions. When you open the configuration screen, you will need to click the buttons to turn these on in your device Settings:

1. **Accessibility Service:** This is the most crucial permission. It is what actually allows SynapseClick to simulate tapping and swiping the screen on your behalf. *(Note: We only use this permission to perform the precise automated clicks you configure!)*
2. **Draw Over Other Apps (Overlay):** This permission allows our transparent floating control menu to stay consistently visible on top of your screen while you are fully immersed inside your games or other active apps.
